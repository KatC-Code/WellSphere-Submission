# WellSphere

## Overview
WellSphere is a holistic wellness tracking app designed to integrate physical, emotional, and mental health data into one supportive platform.  
It combines empathetic prompts, behavioral science, and advanced analytics to provide personalized insights and encourage sustainable lifestyle habits.

## Key Features
- **Comprehensive tracking** of physical, emotional, and mental well‑being  
- **Empathetic prompts** that motivate without overwhelming    
- **Advanced analytics** for personalized insights and habit formation  
- **Clean, user‑friendly interface** that makes wellness data accessible and actionable  

## Purpose
WellSphere isn’t just about monitoring wellness, it’s about empowering users to build sustainable habits, celebrate small wins, and maintain balance across all dimensions of health.
